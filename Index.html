About Me

Hello! My name is Samarventhan Surendran, and I’m a third-year Software Engineering student with CO-OP at the University of Ottawa. I’ve always been passionate about technology and problem-solving, and recently I’ve grown to develop an interest in design and user experience. Outside of academics, I enjoy working on side projects, volunteering in my community, and being involved with student organizations. I’m also a co-founder of the uOttawa Tamil Alliance of Networking (UTAN) club, where we build community among Tamil students through professional development and cultural engagement.

How I Work

I am currently taking a SEG 3125 which is a UI Design course where I am learning the foundational elements of user interface and user experience design. Through this course, I’ve been exploring design elements and principles such as Scale, Visual hierarchy, Balance, Contrast and Gestalt.
